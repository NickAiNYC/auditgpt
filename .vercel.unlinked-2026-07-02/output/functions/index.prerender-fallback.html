The Product

The report is the interface.

AuditGPT is not trying to impress visitors with moving dashboards. The product is a clear review document: claims, evidence, gaps, source URLs, and safer language.

Exhibit 1

Claim review

A structured record of what the page says, where it appears, and what kind of claim it is.

Exhibit 3

Before-and-after language that preserves the business intent while reducing unsupported claims.

Methodology

Evidence first. Language second.

Every review asks the same question: can a reasonable buyer see enough evidence to support the claim without doing investigative work?

01

Extract

Pull every factual, comparative, performance, trust, and compliance claim from the public page.

02

Classify

Label each claim as supported, weakly supported, overstated, unsupported, or AI-distorted.

03

Verify proximity

Check whether evidence is visible near the claim instead of hidden in a distant page or deck.

04

Rewrite

Return safer language, proof-block recommendations, and a dated review receipt.
