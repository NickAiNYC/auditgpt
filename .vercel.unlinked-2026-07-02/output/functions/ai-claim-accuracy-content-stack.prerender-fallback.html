AuditGPT position
AuditGPT is the claim intelligence layer for the AI content stack.

Visibility tools answer: “Does AI mention us?” AuditGPT answers: “Are the claims AI reads and repeats supported enough for buyers, agencies, diligence teams, and regulators to trust?”

Four acquisition-oriented moves

Build the claim graph

Every audit should become structured data: claim text, treatment category, risk label, evidence type, missing proof, safer rewrite, AI distortion, source URL, review date, and cleanup status.

Own the pre-publish gate

The product should move from pull to push: Chrome extension, CMS plugin, and one-button receipt generation before a high-claim page goes live.

Verticalize healthcare first

GLP-1, injectables, body contouring, IV therapy, exosomes, and RF microneedling create the sharpest claim-risk taxonomy and the clearest acquisition story.

Sell diligence

PE rollups, franchisors, and acquirers need pre-acquisition claim risk memos. That is higher ACV than a clinic audit and feeds the same dataset.

Dataset monetization

The dataset should become a product, not just a byproduct.

After enough audits, AuditGPT can sell comparison intelligence back to operators: how a clinic's GLP-1, injector, device, or wellness claims compare to local competitors and category norms.
