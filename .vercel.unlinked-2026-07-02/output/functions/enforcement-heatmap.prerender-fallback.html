Which states are enforcing med-spa claims right now?

State medical boards and task forces are moving faster than most operators expect. New York alone inspected 223 med-spas and cited 87 under its laser-spa enforcement statute (NY 223/87) — and that is just one state. The regulatory patchwork is widening, and enforcement risk is no longer theoretical.
