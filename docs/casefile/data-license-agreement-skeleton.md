**1. Parties & Definitions.**
"Scrutexity" — [entity name, state]. "Customer" — the licensed carrier, TPA, or firm.
**"Licensed Data"** — the contents of any CaseFile bundle and coverage-check response
delivered under this Agreement. **"CaseFile"** — a dated evidence bundle conforming to the
CaseFile Specification v1.0, comprising observations of publicly accessible statements and
publicly obtained AI-system outputs, with integrity proofs. **"Coverage Check"** — a metered
API response stating whether and since when records exist for a domain. **"Methodology"** —
the Scrutexity Records Integrity Methodology, version stated in each bundle, incorporated by
reference.

**2. License Grant & Permitted Uses.** Non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use
Licensed Data solely for: (a) internal claims handling, investigation, and reserving;
(b) litigation and dispute support in matters involving the subject entity, including
production to tribunals, opposing parties, and retained experts under applicable protective
procedures; (c) internal underwriting and portfolio analysis. Copies for these purposes
permitted with integrity sections intact.

**3. Prohibited Uses.** Customer shall not: (a) use Licensed Data as the sole basis for any
decision about a consumer's eligibility for insurance, credit, employment, or housing, or
otherwise use it as a "consumer report"; (b) train, fine-tune, or evaluate machine-learning
models on Licensed Data; (c) resell, sublicense, publish, or publicly disclose Licensed Data
except as permitted in §2(b); (d) represent Licensed Data as a legal conclusion, regulatory
finding, or determination of truth or falsity by Scrutexity; (e) remove or alter integrity
proofs, disclaimers, or the scope statement.

**4. Delivery & Refresh.** Standard CaseFiles delivered within [5] business days of order;
Expedited within [48] hours. Bundles reflect records as of generation time. A one-time
refresh of a purchased CaseFile within [90] days is included; ongoing observation of a
subject requires a monitoring order. No retroactive coverage: Scrutexity has records only
from the first-observed date returned by a Coverage Check.

**5. Fees & Payment.** Per-file: Standard $[1,500]; Expedited with Declaration $[5,000].
Desk subscription: $[25,000]/year including [10] Standard CaseFiles and unlimited Coverage
Checks. Non-subscriber Coverage Checks: $[50–150]/lookup, invoiced monthly, [$500] monthly
minimum for API access. Volume schedule per Exhibit A. Net [30].

**6. Warranties & Disclaimers.** Scrutexity warrants only that Licensed Data was produced
and preserved in conformance with the Methodology and that stated digests verify as
described. EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY STATED, LICENSED DATA IS PROVIDED "AS IS." SCRUTEXITY MAKES
NO WARRANTY AS TO THE TRUTH, ACCURACY, COMPLETENESS, OR LEGAL SIGNIFICANCE OF ANY RECORDED
STATEMENT; NO WARRANTY OF FITNESS FOR ANY PARTICULAR CLAIM DETERMINATION; AND LICENSED DATA
IS NOT LEGAL, MEDICAL, REGULATORY, UNDERWRITING, OR INVESTMENT ADVICE. Coverage gaps are
disclosed in each bundle; absence of a record is not evidence a statement was never made.

**7. Liability Cap & Indemnity.** Scrutexity's aggregate liability capped at fees paid in
the [12] months preceding the claim; no indirect or consequential damages. Customer
indemnifies Scrutexity against claims arising from Customer's use of Licensed Data in
violation of §2–§3, including any use in consumer-eligibility decisions. Scrutexity
indemnifies Customer against third-party claims that the Methodology-conformant production
of Licensed Data infringes intellectual-property rights. [No indemnity by Scrutexity for
defamation claims arising from Customer's disclosure of Licensed Data beyond §2(b) uses.]

**8. Audit & Termination.** Scrutexity may audit API usage against metering records on [10]
days' notice, limited to usage logs. Either party may terminate on [30] days' notice;
Scrutexity may suspend API access immediately on §3 breach. Delivered CaseFiles and rights
in pending litigation survive termination; API access does not.

**9. No-Advice / Independent Judgment.** Customer acknowledges that Licensed Data records
observations of public statements at points in time; that Scrutexity exercises no judgment
about coverage, liability, truth, or compliance; that all claims decisions, reserving
decisions, and litigation positions are Customer's alone, made through Customer's own
qualified personnel and counsel; and that no Scrutexity output shall be cited as a finding
that any person or entity violated any law or standard. This section survives termination.
