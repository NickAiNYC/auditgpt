All emails obey the language doctrine: observed / recorded / dated snapshot / verifiable
record. Never: violation, compliant, certified, safe, guaranteed. Every claim in every email
must trace to a real record before sending — if the bracketed fact doesn't exist yet, the
email doesn't go out.
