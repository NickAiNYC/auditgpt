# Batch 1 — First 10 AI/SaaS Founder Audit Prep

**Goal:** Before sending any DM, manually capture one claim gap, one visibility gap, one demand gap, and a one-sentence DM angle per prospect. Use these to personalize the §1 message in `outbound-messages.md`.

**Do not auto-send.** Fill these in for founder review first.

**Source candidates from:**
- Product Hunt (last 90 days, AI / B2B / SaaS)
- YC W26 / S26 batch pages
- BetaList recent launches
- "Best AI [category]" lists on G2, BetterAI, Latent Space
- Build-in-public X founders
- AI Tools Directory / There's An AI For That

**Qualifies if:**
- Launched in last 18 months or raised publicly in last 12 months
- Has homepage + at least 3 of: pricing page, comparison page, security/trust page
- Uses ≥1 of: "AI," "agent," "autonomous," "enterprise-grade," "SOC 2," "guaranteed," "productivity," "replace," "10x"
- Founder findable on X or LinkedIn with >500 followers
- <500 employees, founder-led buying

**Disqualifies if:**
- Enterprise-sales-only ("Contact us for a quote" with no pricing)
- US-state-regulated category
- Publicly traded
- Existing Gartner/Forrester analyst relationship visible
