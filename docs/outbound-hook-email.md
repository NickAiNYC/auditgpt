# Outbound Hook Emails (A/B/C Testing)

For the Week 4 Sprint, test these three hooks evenly across your first 20 manual scans to determine the strongest response trigger.

---

I found a few signals worth looking at and put them into a short Claim Drift baseline.

This is a public-content diagnostic only, not legal, medical, clinical, or regulatory advice.

Should I send it over?

Nick
